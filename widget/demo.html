How to Embed

1 Install the Widget

Add the BabelBridge script to your website's HTML:

<script src="https://your-domain.com/babelbridge.js"></script>

2 Initialize

Configure your widget with just a few options:

BabelBridge.init({ apiUrl: 'http://localhost:3000', position: 'bottom-right', theme: 'dark', lang: 'en' });

3 Start Chatting!

Your customers can now chat in their preferred language. Try it now by clicking the chat button in the bottom-right corner!
